# Introduction
Borel $\sigma$-algebra ([[Sigma algebra|link]]) on $\mathbb{R}$ is the smallest $\sigma$-algebra containing all open intervals $(a, b)$. It is denoted as $\mathcal{B}(\mathbb{R})$.
