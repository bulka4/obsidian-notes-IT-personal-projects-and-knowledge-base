# Introduction
A ceil of a number is denoted and defined as:
$$
\large
\left\lceil x \right\rceil = \min_y \{y: y \ge x \wedge y \in \mathbb{N}\}
$$
So that is the smallest natural number not lower than given number.

Relative concept is a floor of a number - [[Floor of a number]].
