# Introduction
Diagonal matrix is a square matrix which has zeros everywhere except for the diagonal:
$$
A = \text{diag}(a_{ii})_{i=1}^n = \begin{bmatrix}
a_{11} & 0 & \dots & \dots & 0 \\
0 & a_{22} & 0 & \ldots & 0 \\
\vdots & 0 & \ddots & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & \ldots & \ddots & \ddots & 0 \\
0 & \dots & \dots & 0 & a_{nn} \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
