# Introduction
Domain-specific LLMs are specialized large language models tailored for particular industries—such as legal, finance, healthcare, or IT—by training or fine-tuning on domain-specific datasets. 

They offer higher accuracy, reduced hallucinations, better understanding of specialized jargon, and improved data security compared to general-purpose models
