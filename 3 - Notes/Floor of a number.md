# Introduction
A floor of a number is denoted and defined as:
$$
\large
\left\lfloor x \right\rfloor = \max_y \{y: y \le x \wedge y \in \mathbb{N}\}
$$
So that is the biggest natural number not greater than given number.

Relative concept is a ceil of a number - [[Ceil of a number]].
