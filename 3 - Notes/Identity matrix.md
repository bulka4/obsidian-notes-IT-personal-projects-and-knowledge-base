# Introduction
Identity matrix is a diagonal matrix ([[Diagonal matrix|link]]) which has ones on the diagonal:
$$
I_n = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 & \ldots & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 & \ldots & 0 \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\
0 & \ldots & \ldots & 0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$
and lower index $n$ indicates the size of this matrix (which is square).
