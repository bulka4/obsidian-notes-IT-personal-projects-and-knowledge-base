# Introduction
This document described properties of the Kronecker product ([[Kronecker product|link]]).
# Transpose
$$
(A \otimes B)^T = A^T \otimes B^T
$$
# Matrix vectorization identity
For matrices $A, B$, we have the following identities:
$$
\begin{align}
& \text{vec}(AXB^T) = (B \otimes A)\text{vec}(X) \\
& \text{vec}(AB) = \text{vec}(IAB) = (B^T \otimes I)\text{vec}(A) \\
& \text{vec}(AB) = \text{vec}(ABI) = (I \otimes A)\text{vec}(B) \\
\end{align}
$$
More information about it can be found here - [[Matrix vectorization identity]].
