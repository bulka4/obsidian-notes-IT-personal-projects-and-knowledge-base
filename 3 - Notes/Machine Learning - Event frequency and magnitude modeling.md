# Introduction
There are models allowing to model:
- Event frequency - how often events occur, how likely they are to occur after some time (e.g. how frequently a customer will be buying products)
- Event magnitude - Some quantity related to an event (e.g. how much money a customer will spend when they but a product)

Models for that include:
- BG/NBD (Event frequency)
- Gamma-Gamma (Event magnitude)

More information about this method is here - [kaggle.com](https://www.kaggle.com/code/kemalgunay/cltv-customer-lifetime-value-method).
