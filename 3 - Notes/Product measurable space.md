# Introduction
Let $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ be sets ([[Set|link]]) and $\mathcal{F}_1, \ldots, \mathcal{F}_n$ be a $\sigma$-algebras ([[Sigma algebra|link]]) on them. 

The pair $(\prod_{i=1}^n X_i, \bigotimes_{i=1}^n \mathcal{F}_i)$ is called a product measurable space, where $\bigotimes_{i=1}^n \mathcal{F}_i$ is a product $\sigma$-algebra ([[Product sigma algebra|link]]) on $\prod_{i=1}^n X_i$.
