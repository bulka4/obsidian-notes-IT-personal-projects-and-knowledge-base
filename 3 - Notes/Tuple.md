# Introduction
A tuple is an ordered list of elements of a fixed length:
$$
\large
(x_1, \dots, x_n) \in \prod_{i=1}^n \mathcal{X}_i
$$
Where $\prod_{i=1}^n \mathcal{X}_i$ is a cartesian product ([[Cartesian product|link]]) and each element $x_j$ is an output of a function:
$$
t(j) = x_j \in \mathcal{X}_j
$$
where:
$$
\large
t: \{1, \dots, n\} \rightarrow \bigcup_{j=1}^n \mathcal{X}_j
$$
